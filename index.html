Welcome to Thrain Stonebinder's Armory
Forged in Flame. Bound by Gem. Worn by Legends.

Deep beneath the granite halls of Thunderdeep Hold, Master Armorer Thrain Stonebinder hammers steel not just with fire and forge, but with ancient arcana and the brilliance of charged gemstones. Each suit of armor that leaves his anvil is more than a barrier—it’s a bond between warrior and stone, spirit and steel.

Unlike the clanking contraptions of surface tinkers, Thrain’s creations are a harmony of dwarven metallurgy and arcane resonance. At the heart of every cuirass or gauntlet lies a gem, carefully chosen and magically charged to enhance the wearer’s strength, resilience, or clarity of mind. No two suits are alike; each is custom-fitted, rune-sealed, and soul-attuned.

Whether you're a sellsword heading into the Underdark or a king’s champion defending the realm, Thrain has the armor to match your mettle.
